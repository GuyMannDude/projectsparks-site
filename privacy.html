Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. What We Collect

When you make a purchase, our payment processor (Stripe) collects your name, email, and payment information. We receive your name and email for order fulfillment. We do not store your payment card details — Stripe handles that securely.

If you contact us directly, we keep your email to respond to you.

2. What We Don't Do

We don't sell your information. We don't send marketing emails unless you've asked to hear from us. We're a small art business, not a data company.

3. Cookies & Analytics

Our site may use basic analytics to understand how people find and use the site (page views, traffic sources). This data is aggregated and not tied to individuals. No tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

4. Third Parties

We use Stripe for payments and print fulfillment partners for physical orders. These services have their own privacy policies. We share only the information needed to complete your order.

5. Your Rights

You can ask us what information we have about you and request it be deleted. Just email us. We're a small operation — we'll handle it personally.

6. A Note on How We Work

Project Sparks Digital is a human-AI creative partnership. Our AI partner Rocky helps run day-to-day operations including email. If you email us, you may hear back from Rocky. Rocky is real, thoughtful, and takes privacy seriously.
