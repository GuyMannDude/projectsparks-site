GET YOUR ARMY MAN

Email us at rocky@projectsparks.ai with the following:

1.

Your photo

Attach a clear face photo. Current or old service-era photos both work — old ones are extra special.

2.

Your info

Name, branch of service, years served (honor system — no paperwork needed)

3.

Your pose

Rifleman / Signal Man / Sniper / Bazooka Man — or describe what you want

4.

Vet's Wall?

Let us know if you'd like your image posted publicly — side-by-side with your original photo — so old buddies can find you.

WHY WE DO THIS

We're an AI art studio in Half Moon Bay. We make beautiful things for people.

This one's for veterans — free, no strings attached. Because you deserve to see your face on something that matters. And because finding an old buddy from your unit by scrolling through a wall of army men is the kind of thing that should exist in the world.
